Computing prospect on Quantum

By: Lokesh S N, 2nd year

As fresh innovations continue to emerge, the realm of quantum computing is experiencing rapid expansion.The prominent entities in the quantum computing– Google, IBM, Microsoft, and AWS (Amazon) – among which IBM have significant development in quantum technological innovation.

One of the secrets to building the world’s most powerful computer is probably perched by your bathroom sink. Inside the aluminum canister of IBM’s Systems are three cylinders of diminishing circumference,enclose chandelier of looping silver wires with gold plates and quantum chip in the base. To work properly, this chip requires super-cooling to 0.015 kelvin and colder than outer space.

Related Works

In 2019, researchers on the IBM Quantum team invented a metric known as quantum volume to assign a singular, calculatable measurement of a quantum computer’s ability.The quantum volume test asks the quantum computer to run circuit with random gates and measures how often the circuits output the expected outcomes.

IBM has a road map to launch a 1,121-qubit processor this year and, by 2025, surpass 4,000 qubits by creating modular quantum circuits that link multiple processor chips in the same computer. IBM unveiled its new 433-qubit Osprey chip—the world’s most powerful quantum processor, the speed of which, if represented in traditional bits, would far exceed the total number of atoms in the known universe.

Microsoft announced a Azure Quantum Elements, a new system, that seeks to find advancements in chemical and materials science. Along with this, there was the launch of Copilot in Azure Quantum to assist scientists in utilizing natural language to navigate through complex problems in chemistry and materials science.

Over the past few years quantum has moved from footnote to the top of the global security agenda. To date, 17 countries have national quantum strategies and four more are developing them. China has invested an estimated $25 billion in quantum research since the mid-1980s, according to Quantum Computing Report. Its top quantum scientist, Pan Jianwei, led the launch of the world’s first quantum satellite in 2016 and in 2021 unveiled a then record-breaking 56-qubit quantum computer.

Hartmut neven, Google quanta AI's latest speech on the milestones of quantum computing consists of six steps for building up the quantum computers with billions of transistors. They achieved 2 milestones, one which is faster computation than an advanced frontier classical computer. And second one is the quantum error correction which states that the error rate is reduced to one in a billion system processes. Google’s latest iteration of its quantum machine, the Sycamore quantum processor, currently holds 70 qubits. This is a developed one from the 53 qubits of its earlier version. This makes the new processor approximately 241 million times more advanced than the previous model.

The Tata Institute of Fundamental Research(TIFR), Mumbai, has developed a 3-qubit quantum computer based on superconducting qubits, and is preparing to develop a computer with 7 qubits in collaboration with DRDO and TCS.IISER Pune’s I-HUB Quantum Technology Foundation is working on a 20+ qubit quantum computer based on ion traps and another computer based on neutral atoms along with IIT Roorkee and IIT Guwahati.

In conclusion, quantum computing will be a gift to the upcoming generation through which problems can be solved which are unsolvable till now.
