Brainstorming session - Purple Taggers

Date: December 1, 2023 Place: RLHC

QUANTuMIT held the first brainstorming session as a part of the regular activities. The agenda was to brainstorm and hold an enthralling group discussion on “Time Dilation”. The very essence of this theory is paradox, critical and conceptual thinking along with learning rigorous physics fundamentals. The session began with each attendee giving a self-introduction. Then, diving right into the topic, the head of activities Hari Akshay gave a glimpse of time dilation from the perspective of the infamous science-fiction motion picture “Interstellar”. Diagrammatic and interactive explanations intrigued the attendees. It was carried forward by the Vice chairperson Janani, introducing the analytical reasons and ideology behind time dilation and twin paradox. Following her thoughtful teaching, we had the attendees share their takeaways so far and debated on the soundness of various\ methodologies to carry forward this thought process. Thus, the session proved to be a close packed, igniting discussion!
