Mission

To facilitate enhanced understanding about Quantum mechanics

To provide a comprehensive understanding of the foundational principles and algorithms of Quantum computing

To substantiate the logic behind certain myths prevailing in this field

To convene interactive, cognitive and enthralling discussions and theoretical problem solving sessions

Vision

To indulge enginneering students into the world of theoretical aspects of Quantum Mechanics

Motive

What we do?

Brainstorming sessions & Debates

Learning science and technology has to be strategic and novel. We rely on engaging methodologies that teach the concepts and principles of quantum physics in an enthusiastic and inspirational environment, which is why we conduct brainstorming sessions and debates dedicated for hypothetical areas of quantum theory that greatly stimulates the deeper thinking, critical analysis and reasoning skills of the participants, enhancing their understanding on the same. This approach fosters deeper exploration and sparks intellectual curiosity in quantum physics.

Working on practical and simulation models

As a technical community, we realise the significance of hands- on training and exposure to budding tech and advanced tools out there in the industry. We meticulously gather resources and resource people to train our audience on common, yet exemplary tools like Qiskit, for them to witness and physically realise the working conditions and output production of such tools, which aids them to comprehend to the back- end mechanisms and the applications of the same, also making them industry- ready. This hands-on exposure bridges the gap between theory and practice.

Article writing & Quizzes

Our quantum folk are not just thinkers, but also efficient communicators. Article writing is one such form of conveying and sharing a large chunk of knowledge to grow the quantum tribe and their knowledge as well. Quizzing is our traditional technique of flexing the memory after regular sessions and brainstorming sittings, that serves to be a quick pill for better recollection. Articles and quizzing are our standard ways of sharing and sharpening our comprehension and spontaneity on any concepts/ topics.

Thought experimentation & Video lessons

Visualization is one of the most effective media of teaching. Exciting video lessons, enthralling thought experimentations and discussions keep up the enthusiasm and determination to explore more and dwell deeper into the world of insanely true quantum sciences and their correlated fields. With every new thought experimentation thread, our students find themselves at the verge of new realms of theoretical possibilities which also enhances their critical thinking and reasoning skills, thus benefitting the science community with multifaceted technocrats!
